What is a Cooling-Off Period?

A cooling-off period gives buyers time to reconsider after signing a contract, with the right to withdraw without major penalty. It varies by state and doesn't apply to auctions or if waived.

Cooling-off rights provide crucial buyer protection in private sales.

Understanding your cooling-off period can prevent costly mistakes.

State-specific rules apply—always check before signing.

Cooling-Off in Victoria & Queensland (2025)

  • Victoria: 3 business days for residential properties (private sales). Penalty: 0.2% of price if you withdraw.
  • Queensland: 5 business days. Penalty: 0.25% of price.
  • No cooling-off for auction purchases or if waived (common for competitive offers).
  • Starts from the day the contract is signed by both parties.

Use the period wisely for inspections and finance checks.
